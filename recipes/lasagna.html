Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is made with layes of cheese, noodles and ham, making it a delicious dish for your meals!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Noodle

Steps

  1. Add Noodle
  2. Add layer of Ham
  3. Add layer of Cheese
  4. Repeat as much as you want your lasagna bigger
  5. Set the oven to medium for 50 minutes
