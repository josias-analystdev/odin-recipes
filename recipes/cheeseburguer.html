Well made Cheeseburger

Description

Of course it comes with fries! The classic and beloved Cheeseburguer.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Burguer
  • Bread
  • Fries

Steps

  1. Take burguer to the fridge
  2. Put cheese in top of the burguer
  3. Add bread in another oven
  4. Drop the raw cut potatoes in hot oil and wait for 20 minutes
  5. Stick it all togheter and voiala!
